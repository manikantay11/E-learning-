About Us

Web Dev Basics was founded in 2020 by a team of experienced web developers and educators. Our mission is to empower anyone to learn web development skills through accessible and community-driven online courses.

Our Instructors

Sara Lee (Founder, Lead Instructor) has over 5 years experience teaching web development at various coding bootcamps. Her approach focuses on hands-on learning and building real projects.

Ryan Howard (Instructor) has 10+ years working as a freelance web developer and enjoys mentoring others. He focuses on responsive design best practices in his courses.

At Web Dev Basics, we believe web development should be affordable, self-paced, and collaborative. Our project-based courses aim to build both skills and confidence.
